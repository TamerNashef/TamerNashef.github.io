Covid19 Data Analysis

In this project, I conducted an in-depth analysis of COVID-19 pandemic data sourced from Our World in Data, covering the period from January 10, 2020, to March 31, 2024. I began by importing the data in Excel format and transforming it into a structured format by creating SQL views. This process facilitated efficient data handling and manipulation for subsequent analysis. Once the data was prepared, I imported it into Power BI to perform a comprehensive analysis. Power BI's visualization tools enabled me to examine key metrics and trends across various time periods and geographical regions. Through the use of charts, graphs, and other visualizations, I provided insights into the evolution of the pandemic and its impact on different populations and regions. This analytical process helped identify patterns and potential correlations within the dataset.
